LIFE EXPERIENCES — STORIES FROM THE HEART

7827170170 - NCW Helpline:
Connects women directly to police, hospitals, One Stop Centres, and legal authorities for safety, support, and protection in emergencies or personal crises.

1091 - Women Police Helpline:
Provides immediate police assistance to women in distress and is linked to the national Emergency Response Support System (ERSS 112).

108 - Ambulance Helpline:
Offers quick access to ambulance and medical emergency services across India.

181 - Women Helpline:
A 24×7 helpline offering emergency response, counselling, legal guidance, and information on government welfare schemes for women.

112 - National Emergency Number:
A single, all-India emergency number for police, fire, and ambulance services under the Nirbhaya Fund scheme, ensuring rapid response across all states and UTs.
